Hannes Pinoy is a 22 years old Belgian photographer. He started photography at the age of 18. Currently he combines photography with his studies in Visual Arts. He focusses on portrait photography for models. In his work he pursuits the natural beauty of his models along with a touch of fashion. Hannes captures the essence of elegance in his well-balanced and most expressive pictures. His studios are based in Leuven and Gent, Belgium.
